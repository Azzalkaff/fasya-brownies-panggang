# Design Specification

## Brownies Premium Website

Version: 1.0

---

# 1. Design Vision

Website harus memberikan kesan:

* Premium
* Artisan
* Freshly Baked
* Warm
* Trustworthy

Bukan:

* Toko online murah
* Template UMKM generik
* Marketplace clone
* Landing page salesy

Pengguna harus merasa:

"Produk ini dibuat dengan kualitas yang baik dan layak untuk dibeli atau dijadikan hadiah."

---

# 2. Design Principles

## Principle 01

Product First

Produk adalah hero utama.

Foto produk harus menjadi elemen visual paling dominan.

---

## Principle 02

Whitespace Is Luxury

Gunakan ruang kosong yang cukup.

Jangan memenuhi layar dengan terlalu banyak informasi.

---

## Principle 03

One Main Action

Satu CTA utama:

Pesan via WhatsApp

Semua elemen harus mendukung CTA ini.

---

## Principle 04

Visual Trust

Bangun kepercayaan melalui:

* Foto produk
* Testimoni
* Lokasi
* Konsistensi visual

---

## Principle 05

Mobile First

Mayoritas pengunjung datang dari mobile.

Desain harus dimulai dari layar mobile.

# 19. Photography Direction

Photography quality impacts perceived value more than UI.

Priority:

1. Product Photography
2. Packaging Photography
3. Lifestyle Photography

Avoid:

* Low resolution
* Over edited photos
* Artificial stock images

Preferred:

* Natural lighting
* Warm tones
* Real products

---

# 20. UX Success Criteria

User can identify:

* What product is being sold
* Why it is special
* How much it costs
* How to order

Within 10 seconds.

---

# 21. Accessibility

Minimum Requirements

* Semantic HTML
* Proper heading hierarchy
* Keyboard navigation
* Sufficient contrast
* Descriptive alt text

# 22. Performance Goals

# 23. Final Design Statement

Premium brownies made with care, worthy of sharing, gifting, and enjoying with the people that matter most.
